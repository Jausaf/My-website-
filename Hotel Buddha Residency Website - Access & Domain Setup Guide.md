## Step 4: Get a Custom Domain (Optional but Recommended)

The free domain `hotelresidency-a3vyfccy.manus.space` is good, but a custom domain like `hotelbuddharesidency.com` looks more professional.

### Option A: Purchase a Custom Domain Through Manus (Easiest)

**Steps:**
1. Look for the **Management UI** panel (usually on the right side of your screen)
2. Click on **Settings** (gear icon)
3. Click on **Domains** in the left sidebar
4. Click **"Purchase New Domain"** button
5. Search for your desired domain name (e.g., `hotelbuddharesidency.com`)
6. Check availability
7. Select your domain
8. Complete the payment
9. Your custom domain will be active within minutes!

**Popular domain options to try:**
- `hotelbuddharesidency.com`
- `hotelbuddharesidency.in` (for India)
- `buddharesidency.com`
- `buddharesidencylakhisarai.com`

---

### Option B: Use an Existing Domain You Own

If you already own a domain (purchased from GoDaddy, Namecheap, etc.):

**Steps:**
1. Go to **Management UI** → **Settings** → **Domains**
2. Click **"Connect Existing Domain"**
3. Enter your domain name
4. Follow the instructions to update DNS settings at your domain registrar
5. Wait 24-48 hours for DNS to propagate
6. Your custom domain will be active!

## Step 9: Troubleshooting

### Website Not Loading?
1. Check your internet connection
2. Try a different browser
3. Clear your browser cache (Ctrl+Shift+Delete)
4. Try incognito/private mode

### Domain Not Working?
1. Wait 24-48 hours for DNS propagation
2. Check domain settings in Management UI
3. Contact support if issues persist

### Links Not Working?
1. Check navigation menu
2. Verify all links are correct
3. Test on different devices
